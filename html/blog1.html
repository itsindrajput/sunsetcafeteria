Newly Added

Mango Tango Fusion Salad:

Dive into a refreshing symphony of flavors with our Mango Tango Fusion Salad. Crisp mixed greens, juicy mango slices, and cherry tomatoes are tossed with a zesty mango-lime dressing, creating a burst of tropical goodness on your palate. Topped with grilled chicken for a protein boost and garnished with crunchy almond slivers. This is a vibrant explosion of flavors.

Spiced Infusion Sea Bass:

Immerse yourself in aromatic notes of our Spiced Infusion Sea Bass. Fresh fillets of sea bass are delicately seasoned with a blend of Indian spices, including cumin, coriander, and turmeric, then seared to perfection. Served on a bed of saffron-infused basmati rice and accompanied by a side of mint-cilantro chutney, this dish is a celebration of coastal flavors with a touch of Indian flair.
